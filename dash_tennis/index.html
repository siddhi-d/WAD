Tennis Rules

Learn the essential rules of tennis to enhance your understanding of the game.

  • A match is played between two players (singles) or two teams of two players (doubles).
  • The player who wins the most sets wins the match.
  • A set is won by the first player to win six games, with a two-game advantage.
  • The player who serves alternates every game.
  • The ball must not bounce twice on the same side of the court.
